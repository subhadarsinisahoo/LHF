About Founder

Susan Varghese has been witness to a difficult upbringing herself as she is physically challenged (partially blind). This propagated her to take on such a social responsibility. Ever since she has been a child, she has seen her father supporting and encouraging underprivileged children get educated.

Charity and lending a hand has been a part of her life since young. Susan vowed her life to help underprivileged children even as she grew older. Susan strives to make herself better each pasing day.

Apart from being the Managing Trustee, she works for St.Peter’s School where she can provide her services and support to the children who are under privileged in that school.
