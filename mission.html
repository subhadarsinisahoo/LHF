Mission & Objectives

Mission

“To serve and not to be served”

Vision

Lend A Hand currently provides shelter for children, who have been left homeless or pushed in to sexual exploitation.

Lend A Hand Charitable Foundation is working to rescue every child who is suffering from such a circumstances.

Any orphan, unwanted child with disabilities or unsuitable background will be accepted and loved on equal grounds.

We will work on installing skills and knowledge in each child so that they can courageously face the world.

We envision expanding our Foundation to an Old Age home, Home for Physically Challenged; provide Vocational training to women, School for children over the years to come.

We do not wish to turn away from any child or alone person, we can help them and they can be benefited from our Charitable Foundation.

Objective

1) Provide three healthy meals a day
2) Provide Education
3) Show love and acceptance
4) Bring Confidence in Children
5) Make them realize their potential
6) Provide health care when needed
7) Construct a long term relationship and commitment
8) To bring out their hidden talents
